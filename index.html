About Lunar&Mars (LUMA)

Lunar&Mars (LUMA) is a symbolic BEP-20 token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain. Its total fixed supply of 225,384,400 LUMA represents a conceptual value tied to the transition between the Moon and Mars, two milestones in humanity’s path toward space exploration and settlement.

LUMA is designed as a community-oriented, inspirational token: a way to celebrate, support, and discuss the idea of building the next step after a stable presence on the Moon and aiming toward Mars.

Moon → Mars Symbolism

LUMA does not try to represent a precise physical distance or a technical metric. Instead, it captures a symbolic “effort value”: the conceptual journey between a permanent base on the Moon and a future human presence on Mars.

The token embodies:

  • Collective effort and curiosity around space exploration.
  • Technological progress from lunar infrastructure to Martian goals.
  • A shared narrative: Moon as the first step, Mars as the next horizon.

Token Overview

Core Parameters

Name: Lunar&Mars

Symbol: LUMA

Standard: BEP-20 (ERC-20 compatible)

Network: BNB Smart Chain

Decimals: 18

Supply

Total supply: 225,384,400 LUMA

All tokens are initially minted to the deployer wallet. The contract optionally allows controlled minting by the owner and voluntary burns by any holder.

Contract Features

  • mint() – owner-controlled, optional future minting.
  • burn() – any holder can burn their LUMA tokens.
  • Based on OpenZeppelin ERC-20 implementation.
  • No hidden mechanics, no automatic fees or reflections.
