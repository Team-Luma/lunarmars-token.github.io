What is LUMA?

A meme-style token with a simple idea: every holder is one pixel in the corridor that connects our Moon to the red storms of Mars.

In the LUMA lore, the Moon is the first safe harbour: a place where fuel is produced, stations are built and humans learn to live off-world. Mars is the distant horizon: dusty, harsh, but full of stories and future colonies.
