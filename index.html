Mission ‚Äì From the Moon to Mars

LUMA represents the technological, financial and human transition between a sustainable lunar base and the first steps toward a Martian settlement. It is a symbolic bridge between two worlds.

In the story of human space exploration, the Moon is the first safe harbour ‚Äì our advanced base. It is where infrastructure is built, resources are extracted and fuel is produced for deeper missions.

Mars is the distant horizon: a red world to explore, a place for future colonies and a symbol of boldness, innovation and perseverance. LUMA is a token that captures this transition: a corridor of energy, science and community connecting the Moon and Mars.

The total supply of 225,384,400 LUMA is used as a symbolic value for the effort required to cross the gap between these two worlds. It does not represent shares or debt ‚Äì it is a narrative asset for people who believe in an interplanetary future.

Core values Transparency, curiosity, respect for risk and a long-term view of human exploration.

Tokenomics & contract design

A simple, transparent smart contract with mint / burn capabilities and a fixed supply chosen as a symbolic representation of the Moon ‚Üí Mars journey.

  • Network & standard BNB Smart Chain using the BEP-20 standard, compatible with most wallets and DeFi platforms.
  • Total supply Hard-capped at 225,384,400 LUMA, echoing the symbolic bridge between the Moon and Mars.
  • Mint function Minting is restricted to the contract owner to allow controlled experiments. Any policy should be communicated transparently to the community.
  • Burn function Any holder can burn LUMA to reduce circulating supply, leaving a personal on-chain footprint.
  • OpenZeppelin base The contract uses audited OpenZeppelin components for transfers, allowances and standard BEP-20 logic.
