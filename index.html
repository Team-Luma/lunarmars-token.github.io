What is Lunar&Mars (LUMA)?

LUMA is a community-driven token representing the technological, financial and human transition between a sustainable lunar base and the first steps toward a Martian settlement.

The idea behind LUMA is simple: create a clear symbol for the path “Moon → Mars”. Instead of promising returns, the project focuses on storytelling, education and experimentation around space-themed crypto assets.

LUMA can be used as a badge for supporters of human exploration beyond the Moon, a tool for creative projects, or a building block for experiments such as games, NFTs, or community incentives.

The contract is based on OpenZeppelin BEP-20 primitives and deploys on BNB Smart Chain for low fees and easy integration with existing wallets and DeFi tools.

Core values Transparency, curiosity, respect for risk, and a long-term view of human exploration.
Use cases Community rewards, personal collections, experimental dApps, and space-themed projects.
Not a security LUMA does not represent equity, debt, or revenue rights. It is a conceptual and symbolic token.
Token mechanics

A simple smart contract, focused on clarity, with mint and burn functions under owner control and open burning for all holders.

  • Network & standard BNB Smart Chain (BSC) using the BEP-20 standard, compatible with most wallets and DeFi platforms.
  • Total supply Hard-capped at 225,384,400 LUMA, mirroring the symbolic bridge between the Moon and Mars.
  • Mint function Minting is restricted to the contract owner to allow controlled experiments. Policy can be transparently published to the community.
  • Burn function Any holder can burn LUMA to reduce the circulating supply, creating a personal on-chain footprint.
  • OpenZeppelin base The contract uses audited OpenZeppelin components for transfers, allowances and standard BEP-20 logic.

How to get LUMA

LUMA lives on BNB Smart Chain. You can store it in any wallet compatible with BEP-20 tokens. Liquidity and exchange listings are optional and may evolve over time.

  • 1. Install a BSC-compatible wallet such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet, and configure the BNB Smart Chain network.
  • 2. Add the token manually using the contract address 0xEA6C0908734D7cADc10C01b2e4E9d8e1664EECc9 and 18 decimals.
  • 3. Receive LUMA by transfer from the deployer or from community distributions. Always double-check the contract address.
  • 4. (Optional) If liquidity is available, you can trade the LUMA/BNB pool using DEX interfaces such as ApeSpace or Dexscreener. Always verify the contract address and slippage before trading.

FAQ

A few common questions about what LUMA is—and what it is not.

  • Is LUMA an investment? No. LUMA is not marketed as an investment and does not guarantee price or returns. It is a conceptual token created for narrative, experimentation and community use.
  • Who controls the contract? The deployer initially controls the owner functions such as minting. Any future policy, including potential renouncement, should be clearly communicated to the community.
  • Why BNB Smart Chain? Low transaction fees, fast confirmation times and a broad ecosystem of tools make BSC a convenient chain for experimental and community-focused tokens.
  • Can I build with LUMA? Yes. You can create games, NFTs, dApps or educational tools that use LUMA, as long as you clearly state that the token is symbolic and carries no financial guarantees.
